Our Story

Fresh Meat, Honest Prices, Trusted Since Day One

Fresh Meat Shop is a family-run butchery in the heart of Karol Bagh, New Delhi. We wake up early every morning to source the freshest chicken, mutton, and pork directly from trusted farms and local mandis — so you always get meat that's fresh, clean, and full of flavour.

Located at Shop No. 293/4, Padam Singh Road, we've been serving the Regar Pura, Karol Bagh and surrounding Delhi NCR communities with pride. Whether you're a home cook, a restaurant, or a caterer, we cut your meat exactly the way you want it.
